Registration includes a race kit with a bib number, an official race shirt, and other goodies.

Participants can register online.

Aid Stations

Aid stations will be placed every 5 kilometers, providing water, energy drinks, and snacks to keep you fueled throughout the race.

Medical assistance will be available at all aid stations and the finish line.

Cut-off Times

Participants must complete the 50KM course within 10 hours.

Checkpoints along the route will monitor progress to ensure the safety and well-being of all runners.

Safety Guidelines

Runners must wear their bib numbers at all times for identification.

It is recommended to carry a personal water bottle or hydration pack.

Listen to your body and seek medical assistance if you feel unwell during the race.

FINISHER’S SHIRT

All participants who complete the race within the cut-off time will receive an exclusive finisher’s shirt.
