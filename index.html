Registration includes a race kit with a bib number, an official race shirt, and other goodies.

Participants can register online.

Aid Stations

Aid stations will be placed every 5 kilometers, providing water, energy drinks, and snacks to keep you fueled throughout the race.

Medical assistance will be available at all aid stations and the finish line.

Cut-off Times

Participants must complete the 50KM course within 10 hours.

Checkpoints along the route will monitor progress to ensure the safety and well-being of all runners.

Safety Guidelines

Runners must wear their bib numbers at all times for identification.

It is recommended to carry a personal water bottle or hydration pack.

Listen to your body and seek medical assistance if you feel unwell during the race.

FINISHER’S SHIRT

All participants who complete the race within the cut-off time will receive an exclusive finisher’s shirt.

AWARDS AND PRIZES

Top finishers in various categories will receive medals and prizes.Categories include overall male and female finishers

Dear Runners, Supporters, and Friends,

On behalf of the entire race team, I am thrilled to welcome you to the Catanduanes Abaca Ultra 50KM 2025! Whether you are a seasoned ultra-runner or taking on this challenge for the first time, we are honored to have you join us in this exciting journey across the stunning landscapes of Catanduanes.

This race is more than just a test of endurance—it is a celebration of the island’s rich culture, the resilience of its people, and the beauty of its world-renowned abaca industry. As you take on the course, expect breathtaking coastal views, rolling hills, and the warmth of the local community cheering you on every step of the way.

To our dedicated supporters, volunteers, and sponsors, thank you for making this event possible. Your unwavering support ensures that every participant experiences a safe and unforgettable race.
